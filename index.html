Building tech at the intersection of

I'm Franklin Mutuma, a software developer and GIS engineer based in Kenya, working across the water resources, wildlife conservation, and agribusiness sectors. My work spans full-stack web development, Android application engineering, and spatial data infrastructure.

As the founder of Eelam Innovations, I develop technology solutions for organizations like CETRAD, Mikam Water, and community conservation projects across Meru, Isiolo, Samburu, and Laikipia counties.

I have hands-on field experience deploying IoT smart water meters, setting up GIS pipelines with GeoNode and PostgreSQL, and building Android apps with Gemini AI and M-Pesa integrations — from field installation to production deployment.
